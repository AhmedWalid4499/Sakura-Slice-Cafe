Cardamom Maple Milk
Warm, lightly spiced, deeply comforting — a hug made drinkable.
☕ This Sunday · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk — dairy or oat
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • ⅛ tsp ground cardamom
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Tiny splash of vanilla
How to Make It
  1. 01Heat milk gently over low heat — no rushing.
  2. 02Stir in maple syrup, cardamom, cinnamon. Let spices bloom.
  3. 03Add vanilla, pour into your favourite mug.
  4. 04Sit somewhere soft. Hold with both hands. Sip slowly. ☕
"Warmth is patience made drinkable." — Me3temed
🍵
Hojicha Honey Latte
Toasted, earthy, soft — the taste of a slow Japanese morning.
❄️ Winter Favourite · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp hojicha powder or 2 tsp loose leaf
  • ¾ cup steamed oat milk
  • 1 tsp raw honey
  • Pinch of sea salt
How to Make It
  1. 01Brew hojicha in ¼ cup hot water for 3 minutes.
  2. 02Stir in honey until dissolved.
  3. 03Steam or froth oat milk and pour over.
  4. 04Finish with a tiny pinch of sea salt. 🍵
"Toasted mornings taste like permission to slow down." — Me3temed
🌸
Sakura Blossom Tea
Delicate floral warmth. A cup that smells like springtime longing.
🌸 Classic · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp dried sakura blossoms (salted or plain)
  • 1 cup hot water — not boiling, ~80°C
  • ½ tsp honey (optional)
  • Slice of lemon
How to Make It
  1. 01If using salted blossoms, soak in cold water 5 min, then drain.
  2. 02Place blossoms in your cup and pour over hot water.
  3. 03Watch them bloom. Add honey and lemon if you wish.
  4. 04Drink slowly and notice the quiet. 🌸
"Even a single petal can carry the weight of spring." — Me3temed
🍫
Dark Chocolate Miso
Deep, complex, gently salty. Unexpected and unforgettable.
✦ Chef's Pick · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 cup whole milk or oat milk
  • 2 tbsp good dark cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp white miso paste
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or sugar
  • Pinch of chili flakes (optional, but try it)
How to Make It
  1. 01Whisk cocoa, miso, and sweetener with 2 tbsp warm milk into a paste.
  2. 02Heat remaining milk until just steaming.
  3. 03Pour hot milk into the paste, whisk until smooth.
  4. 04Top with chili flakes. Trust the process. 🍫
"Depth needs contrast. Salt makes sweetness real." — Me3temed
🍊
Yuzu Brown Butter Milk
Citrus-bright and nutty. Sunshine in a cup, even in winter.
☀️ Morning Glow · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp yuzu juice (or lemon + tiny orange zest)
  • ½ tsp honey
  • Pinch of vanilla
How to Make It
  1. 01Brown butter in a small pan over medium until nutty and golden.
  2. 02Add milk and heat gently. Stir in honey and vanilla.
  3. 03Remove from heat, add yuzu juice. Froth if desired.
  4. 04Pour into a warm mug. Breathe the citrus in first. ☀️
"Some mornings need a little sunshine engineered into them." — Me3temed
🌙
Moon Milk Blend
Ashwagandha, lavender, oat — brewed for quiet and sleep.
🌙 Evening Only · tap for recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • ½ tsp ashwagandha powder
  • ¼ tsp dried lavender (or 1 drop food-grade oil)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Pinch of nutmeg
How to Make It
  1. 01Warm oat milk over low heat — keep it gentle like the evening.
  2. 02Whisk in ashwagandha, lavender, and honey.
  3. 03Strain through a fine sieve if using dried lavender.
  4. 04Finish with nutmeg. Drink in dim light. Let it work. 🌙
"The night doesn't rush. Neither should you." — Me3temed

Sakura Slice Café was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves to start and end their day with something warm and gentle. Ahmed created the café as a space for slow mornings, honest words, and the kind of care that asks nothing in return.
