Cardamom Maple Milk
Warm, lightly spiced, deeply comforting — a hug made drinkable.
☕ This Week · Tap for recipe
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup milk — dairy or oat
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • ⅛ tsp ground cardamom
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • Tiny splash of vanilla
How to Make It
  1. 01Heat milk gently over low heat — no rushing.
  2. 02Stir in maple syrup, cardamom, cinnamon.
  3. 03Add vanilla. Pour into your favourite mug.
  4. 04Hold with both hands. Sip slowly. ☕
"Warmth is patience made drinkable." — Me3temed
🍵
Hojicha Honey Latte
Toasted, earthy, soft — the taste of a slow Japanese morning.
❄️ Winter Favourite
+
Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp hojicha powder
  • ¾ cup steamed oat milk
  • 1 tsp raw honey
  • Pinch of sea salt
How to Make It
  1. 01Brew hojicha in ¼ cup hot water for 3 min.
  2. 02Stir in honey until dissolved.
  3. 03Steam oat milk and pour over.
  4. 04Finish with a tiny pinch of sea salt. 🍵
"Toasted mornings taste like permission to slow down." — Me3temed

Sakura Blossom Tea
Delicate floral warmth. A cup that smells like springtime longing.
🌸 Classic
+
Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp dried sakura blossoms
  • 1 cup hot water ~80°C
  • ½ tsp honey (optional)
  • Slice of lemon
How to Make It
  1. 01Soak salted blossoms 5 min, drain.
  2. 02Place in cup and pour hot water over.
  3. 03Watch them bloom. Add honey and lemon.
  4. 04Drink slowly and notice the quiet. 🌸
"Even a single petal can carry the weight of spring." — Me3temed

Dark Chocolate Miso
Deep, complex, gently salty. Unexpected and unforgettable.
✦ Chef's Pick
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 2 tbsp dark cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp white miso paste
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • Pinch of chili flakes
How to Make It
  1. 01Whisk cocoa, miso, sweetener with 2 tbsp warm milk into a paste.
  2. 02Heat remaining milk until steaming.
  3. 03Pour hot milk into paste, whisk smooth.
  4. 04Top with chili flakes. Trust the process. 🍫
"Depth needs contrast. Salt makes sweetness real." — Me3temed

Yuzu Brown Butter Milk
Citrus-bright and nutty. Sunshine in a cup, even in winter.
☀️ Morning Glow
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp yuzu juice
  • ½ tsp honey
  • Pinch of vanilla
How to Make It
  1. 01Brown butter in pan until nutty and golden.
  2. 02Add milk, heat gently. Stir in honey and vanilla.
  3. 03Remove from heat, add yuzu juice.
  4. 04Pour into warm mug. Breathe the citrus in first. ☀️
"Some mornings need sunshine engineered into them." — Me3temed

Moon Milk Blend
Ashwagandha, lavender, oat — brewed for quiet and sleep.
🌙 Evening Only
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • ½ tsp ashwagandha powder
  • ¼ tsp dried lavender
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Pinch of nutmeg
How to Make It
  1. 01Warm oat milk over low heat.
  2. 02Whisk in ashwagandha, lavender, honey.
  3. 03Strain through fine sieve.
  4. 04Finish with nutmeg. Drink in dim light. 🌙
"The night doesn't rush. Neither should you." — Me3temed

Matcha Sesame Latte
Earthy, nutty, grounding — made for days that belong entirely to you.
🌿 Saturday Special
+
Ingredients
  • 1 tsp ceremonial matcha
  • ¾ cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp tahini or sesame paste
  • ½ tsp honey
  • Pinch of sea salt
How to Make It
  1. 01Whisk matcha with 2 tbsp warm water until frothy.
  2. 02Blend tahini into warmed oat milk.
  3. 03Combine, sweeten with honey, add salt.
  4. 04Pour over. Sit in the morning light. 🌿
"Matcha tastes like the version of yourself that has enough time." — Me3temed

Vanilla Coconut Steamer
Creamy, sweet, celebratory — a Friday treat for a week well lived.
✨ Friday Special
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Pinch of sea salt
How to Make It
  1. 01Heat coconut milk over gentle heat.
  2. 02Whisk in vanilla, honey, nutmeg and sea salt.
  3. 03Froth until light and creamy.
  4. 04Pour into your favourite mug. You've earned this. ✨
"Fridays taste best when you let them be sweet." — Me3temed

Ginger Persimmon Tea
Bright, warming, gently sweet — autumn bottled and heated with care.
🍂 Seasonal
+
Ingredients
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 slices fresh ginger
  • 1 small ripe persimmon, diced
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Star anise or cinnamon stick
How to Make It
  1. 01Simmer ginger and spices in water 5 min.
  2. 02Add persimmon, simmer 3 more minutes.
  3. 03Strain into a warm mug, stir in honey.
  4. 04Sip slowly. Let the warmth do its work. 🍂
"Autumn always knew how to say goodbye beautifully." — Me3temed
